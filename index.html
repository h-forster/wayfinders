Wayfinders: An Introduction

The Initial Problem

There is currently a major problem in which students experiencing some difficulty in navigating the buildings, more specifically, they are unable to find the rooms they are supposed to be in for their lectures. Students usually have to resort to asking staff or other students walking by for directions.

The Challenge

To create a web application which features an interactive map and allows students an easy way to navigate the different buildings.

Our solution

We have developed a website that allows students to view different rooms in each campus.
